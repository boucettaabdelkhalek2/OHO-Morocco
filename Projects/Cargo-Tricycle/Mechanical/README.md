# Mechanical Design

## 📐 Frame Structure
- Material: Steel (recommended for strength & low cost)
- Type: Rear cargo tricycle (delta configuration)
- Welding joints reinforced at stress points

## 📏 Dimensions (initial prototype)
- Length: 180 – 200 cm
- Width: 80 – 100 cm
- Height: 100 cm
- Cargo area: 60 × 80 cm

## ⚙️ Wheels
- Front: 26 inch (steering)
- Rear: 20–24 inch (load support)
- Tires: reinforced (high load capacity)

## 📦 Cargo Box
- Material: wood or lightweight steel
- Max load: 80–150 kg

## ⚖️ Stability
- Low center of gravity
- Wide rear axle
- Even weight distribution
