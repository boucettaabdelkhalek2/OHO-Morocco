# 🔩 Manufacturing Notes

## Frame Geometry
- Main angle: ~6° steering tilt
- Steel tube: 20x20mm recommended
- Welding type: MIG/MAG

## Critical Points
- Steering joint must be reinforced
- Front wheel mounts must be perfectly parallel
- Cargo box must be welded after alignment check

## Assembly Strategy
1. Dry fit all parts
2. Tack weld structure
3. Verify geometry
4. Final welding
5. Surface finishing
