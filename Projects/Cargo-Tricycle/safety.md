# ⚠️ Safety Guidelines – Cargo Tricycle

## Welding Safety
- Always wear welding mask (DIN 11+)
- Use gloves and protective clothing
- Work in ventilated area

## Structural Safety
- Check frame alignment before final welding
- Test steering movement before load testing

## Load Safety
- Maximum load: 150kg
- Do not exceed recommended center of gravity

## Testing Phase
- Always test without cargo first
- Perform brake test before road use
